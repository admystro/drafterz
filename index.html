Team

We are a team from Ukraine with our main office in Kyiv. Our artists are located not only in different cities of our country, but also in different countries. The infrastructure is built in such a way as to diversify risks, taking into account the times we live in.

We have been providing VFX services for over 5 years. For more details, please see the services section. Our main advantages are our human approach to all people and our adherence to deadlines.

Neither COVID-19 with its quarantines, nor any military actions and their consequences have prevented us from delivering our work on time and in the agreed quality, and sometimes even better and faster.

Of course, this comes at a price, but our flexible pricing system and lack of fixed rates allow us to be loyal, especially in the case of long-term cooperation.

Clients from the following countries have repeatedly placed their trust in us: England, Hungary, Spain, Israel, Kazakhstan, Canada, Lithuania, Malta, Mexico, the Netherlands, the UAE, Serbia, Saudi Arabia, the USA, Ukraine, the Czech Republic, Sweden, Japan and several others.

If you are not located in a country subject to international sanctions, please contact us via email in the contact section to discuss cooperation.

Works

Simulations (FX/Dynamics): Creation of realistic physical phenomena: fire, smoke, water, explosions, destruction, fabric, hair, particles.

Compositing: Combining rendered 3D elements, live footage, and other layers into a single whole (e.g., integrating CG objects into a frame).

3D Modelling: Creating 3D objects, characters, and environments.

Texturing & Shading: Assigning materials to surfaces, adjusting their interaction with light (SSS, IOR, AO).

Lighting: Placing light sources, adjusting their parameters to create atmosphere and realism.

Animation: Bringing characters, objects, and cameras to life (including motion design work).

Tracking: Tracking the movement of cameras and objects in real video to insert CG elements.

Rotoscoping: Outlining objects to separate them from the background.

Motion Capture/Mocap: Transferring an actor's movements to a 3D character.

This is far from everything we do. To gain a broader understanding of how we can be of service to you, please refer to the information in the services section.
